About Us

Welcome to LPP, where every slice tells a story! Founded by pizza lovers with a dream, our journey began with a commitment to bring authentic, handcrafted pizzas to our community.

Little Pizza Place is more than just a place to eat it's a hub for friends and family to gather, celebrate, and make memories. We actively support local farmers and participate in community events, believing in giving back to the neighborhood that supports us.

At Little Pizza Shop, we're serving happiness, one slice at a time!

Did you know?

Our first pizza was made in a small home kitchen back in 2015!
